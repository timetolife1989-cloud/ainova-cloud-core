# Ainova Cloud Intelligence — Market Analysis

## 1. Global MES/MOM Market

### 1.1 Market Size
- **2024**: $14.2 billion (global MES market)
- **2030 forecast**: $26.1 billion (CAGR: 10.7%)
- **Europe**: ~35% of global market
- **CEE Region**: Fastest growing segment (12-15% CAGR)

### 1.2 Key Drivers
- **Industry 4.0 adoption** — Digital transformation in manufacturing
- **GDPR compliance** — On-premise deployment demand in EU
- **SME digitalization** — Gap in affordable manufacturing software
- **Supply chain resilience** — Post-pandemic focus on visibility
- **ESG reporting** — Environmental compliance tracking needs

---

## 2. Target Market

### 2.1 Primary Segment
**SME manufacturers** (50-500 employees) in Central and Western Europe:
- Automotive suppliers
- Electronics assembly
- Food & beverage production
- Metal fabrication & machining
- Plastics & packaging

### 2.2 Pain Points
1. **Overpriced enterprise MES** — SAP ME, Siemens Opcenter cost €50K-500K+
2. **Feature bloat** — Enterprise solutions include 80% unused features
3. **Complex deployment** — 6-12 month implementation cycles
4. **Vendor lock-in** — Proprietary databases, closed ecosystems
5. **No cloud option** — Legacy on-premise only architecture

- ✅ **Modern tech**: Next.js, React, real-time updates
